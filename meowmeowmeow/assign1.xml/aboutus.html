About Us

Carry Mi Senpai~

RV Senpai is a company providing Recreational vehicles rental service in Malaysia founded in year 2008, in Kuching, by Mr.Ericson Tan. The reason he started this business is because he was inspire by the self-driving travel style in the western country during his study time and he thinks that it would be perfect for this services to be implement in Malaysia. The company is first started in Kuching Sarawak with the amount of only 10 standard model RVs. Throughout the years, the company is growing rapidly until now we have business links that cover the whole East Malaysia and also providing a variety types of Recreational vehicles and other outdoor camping equipments.
